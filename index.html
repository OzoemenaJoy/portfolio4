Welcome to my digital space! I’m Ifunanya Joy, a passionate UI/UX designer and frontend developer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creating seamless digital experiences. With a strong foundation in both design and development, I bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality to craft intuitive, engaging, and user-centric interfaces. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and front-end coding to bring creative concepts to life. My approach is deeply rooted in understanding user needs and leveraging cutting-edge technologies to build responsive, accessible, and visually stunning web applications. In my design work, I strive to create visually compelling interfaces that are not only beautiful but also functional and user-friendly. From crafting detailed wireframes and interactive prototypes to designing pixel-perfect UI elements, I focus on delivering designs that solve real user problems and enhance the overall user experience. On the development side,My expertise spans HTML and CSS. I transform designs into dynamic, visually appealling web applications using modern front-end technologies.
